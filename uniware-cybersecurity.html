Where to start

Four levels. Each one right for a different situation.

Security is not one size fits all. The right level depends on your risk exposure, your industry, and your budget. Each level builds on the one below it.

MANAGED
ADVANCED
ESSENTIAL
FOUNDATION

Level 1 — Foundation

The minimum every business should have.

A properly configured and fully managed firewall. Perimeter security and traffic monitoring. Without this in place, every other layer is more vulnerable.

Best for

Every business, regardless of size.

Level 2 — Essential

The three most common attack routes, covered.

Everything in Foundation, plus endpoint protection across all devices, email security, and a backup plan for data recovery.

Best for

Growing businesses. Typically 20 to 200 users.

Level 3 — Advanced

Control at every point, and a backup no attacker can touch.

Everything in Essential, plus zero-trust access control, data loss prevention, mobile device management, immutable backup, and vulnerability assessment.

Best for

Businesses handling sensitive data or with compliance requirements. Typically 50 to 500 users.

Level 4 — Managed

Expert eyes on your environment, around the clock.

Everything in Advanced, plus 24/7 monitoring and incident response through specialist security partners.

Best for

Businesses in higher-risk industries, or those requiring constant coverage.
